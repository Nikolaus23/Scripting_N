Who Is Nikolaus?

Nikolaus is a student at Lethbridge College, taking two classes per semester as a way to learn at a pace that works for him. He joined the Multimedia Program to improve his understanding of photography. He wishes to use photography in a future career but is also willing to see what other options will be open to him after college.

About Nikolaus Work and Education.

What Work I Do

Nikolaus enjoys taking photos around his home whenever he has an interesting idea for a photo which he sometimes finds hard to do. Then he uses Photoshop to edit them to see how he can improve his work, by evaluating how colour, lighting, and other factors can change a photo.

About my Education

Nikolaus won an award for an occupational component in Victoria Park for his work in photography at the school. His favourite course before going to college was probably Science / Biology as he loves to learn more about how the world works, or at least how we think it works.

A Fact About Me

One time, when he was young, Nikolaus fell off his bed in the middle of the night and woke up due to the pain. His cry woke up his parents who told him to go back to sleep, and it was only later in the day did they find out that he broke his collarbone because of that fall.
