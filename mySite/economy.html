Economy

Farming and Potash

Despite its size, Biggar plays an essential role in the Saskatchewan economy. Biggar has at least 41 farms making up about 8 440 acres or 3 416 hectares of land, showing the importance of agriculture in Biggar. Biggar is also an hour away from two potash mines in Saskatchewan, Cory Potash and Agrium Potash, making the town an important home to the labour force.

Malthouse

Also, Biggar is home to the Boortmalt malthouse opened in 1977, it is the company's biggest malthouse. This shows that even though this town is not huge, it still has an important place in Saskatchewan and will always have a bigger impact than its size might suggest.
